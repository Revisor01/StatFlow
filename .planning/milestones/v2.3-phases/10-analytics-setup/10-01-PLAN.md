**Inhalt der Sektionen (englische Strings, DE-Uebersetzung kommt in Task 2):**

**1. Umami Setup (orange, "chart.bar.xaxis"):**
- Schritt 1: "Log in to Umami" — Open your Umami instance, go to Settings > Websites, click "Add website". Enter name and domain.
- Schritt 2: "Copy the tracking script" — After adding the website, click the tracking code button. You'll see a `<script>` tag.
- Schritt 3: "Add to your website" — Paste the script before the closing `</head>` tag of every page. For WordPress: use a plugin like "Insert Headers and Footers". For Next.js: add to `_document.tsx` or `app/layout.tsx`.
- Schritt 4: "Visit your website" — Open your website in a browser. Within a minute, Umami should show your first pageview.

Zeige diesen Code-Block bei Schritt 3:
```
<script defer
  src="https://your-umami.example/script.js"
  data-website-id="YOUR-WEBSITE-ID">
</script>
```

**2. Plausible Setup (blue, "chart.line.uptrend.xyaxis"):**
- Schritt 1: "Log in to Plausible" — Go to your Plausible instance, click "+ Add website". Enter your domain (without https://).
- Schritt 2: "Copy the snippet" — Plausible shows you a one-line script snippet.
- Schritt 3: "Add to your website" — Paste before `</head>`. For WordPress: use "Insert Headers and Footers". For Hugo/Jekyll: add to base layout. For Webflow: Settings > Custom Code > Head code.
- Schritt 4: "Verify in Plausible" — Click "Start collecting data". Plausible shows a green checkmark once it detects traffic.

Zeige diesen Code-Block bei Schritt 3:
```
<script defer
  data-domain="yourdomain.com"
  src="https://your-plausible.example/js/script.js">
</script>
```

**3. Goals definieren (purple, "target"):**

Unterteile diese Sektion in zwei Sub-Abschnitte mit kleinen Ueberschriften:

*Umami Goals:*
- "In Umami, go to Settings > Websites > your website > Goals"
- "Click 'Add goal'. Choose type: URL (for page visits) or Event (for custom events)"
- "For a URL goal: enter the path, e.g. /thank-you or /checkout/success"
- "For an event goal: enter the event name you send from your code (e.g. 'signup', 'purchase')"

*Plausible Goals:*
- "In Plausible, go to your website settings > Goals > + Add goal"
- "Choose Pageview (URL path) or Custom Event"
- "Pageview example: /thank-you — fires when someone visits that page"
- "Custom Event: add the Plausible events script and trigger events from JavaScript"

**4. Datenfluss pruefen (green, "checkmark.circle"):**
- "Open this app and pull to refresh on any website"
- "Check that pageview numbers increase after you visit your tracked website"
- "For Goals: wait up to 10 minutes after a conversion. Goals appear in the Goals section of the website detail view."
- "If no data appears: check that the script is loading (browser DevTools > Network tab, look for script.js). Verify the website ID or domain matches exactly."

**Styling-Hinweise:**
- GuideStep: Nummer in einem `.fill`-Kreis (Systemfarbe `.blue`/`.orange`/etc passend zur Sektion), Titel `.headline`, Beschreibung `.body` in `.secondary`
- CodeBlock: `.monospaced`-Font, `.gray.opacity(0.12)` Hintergrund, `RoundedRectangle(cornerRadius: 8)`, `.padding(12)`, horizontaler Scroll via `ScrollView(.horizontal)`
- GuideSectionHeader: Icon in gefaerbtem RoundedRectangle (wie in SettingsView), Titel `.title2 .bold`
- Zwischen Sektionen: `Divider()` mit `.padding(.vertical, 8)`
